Bio

YO! My name is Quincy Washington, I'm a 20 year old New Media major at SUNY Purchase College. I'm a self proclaimed artist, I've been doing art since I could pick up a pencil. I draw, paint, do digital art, and I guess coding too. I've worked with an organization called Groundswell that allowed me to paint 6 murals with them in different boroughs in Brooklyn and the Bronx. My goal for the future is to graduate college, get my masters and... well my career path is still a mystery to me.
