Graphic Design

Word. Meaning. Play.

For my Design Principles Midterm the prompt was to find 9 original words and then choose the color, typeface and background. Then the main part of the project was to express the meaning or idea of the words using size, placement or spacing on the page.

Poster Project

For this Design Principles assignment the prompt was centered around the phrase "Students are workers", we were meant to come up with a subtitle for that phrase and create a poster to go alongside it. The first poster on the left was my final design, while the other two were just concept ideas that I liked enough to flesh out into final posters as well.
