Changes

For my Intro to Sound midterm I wanted to use common sounds that I found around me, like microwave beeps, paper ripping, drawers opening and closing, etc., and use them to create a beat. While that was my main premise, I wanted to take that a step further and add a narrative. I used recordings of quotes from movies and TV shows and put them together in order to create a 'story'. In this story the voices are convincing me to make my beat more complex until they're satisfied.

Eye of the Tiger Remix

For my Intro to Sound final I wanted to take a step back and focus on one thing in order to really put all my effort into it. For this project I took the idea of using common sounds to create a beat and took that to the next level. I recreated the song Eye of the Tiger by Survivor using only my own recorded sounds, including finger tapping, drawer closing, microwave beeps, paper crumbling, etc. and then I changed each individual recording's speed, tone, volume and length to match the sounds in the actual song and repeated them when necessary and then put it all together for this DIY version of the well known classic Eye of the Tiger.
